AWENS Safety Standards

Updated: September 1st, 2020

Providing Security

AWENSâ€™ staff is committed to providing a safe learning environment during this pandemic. Currently, our education centre is open to our clients. We follow the current guidelines set by the provincial health authority and continue to update as the situation evolves.

Our Plan

  • Our classrooms have been modified to ensure social distancing at all times.
  • Each training room has a separate enter and exit door to ensure a safe entry and exit from every room
  • Gloves, masks, and sanitizer will be provided to instructors, employees and students as necessary
  • Training rooms will be sanitized before and after each class
  • Our classes and ISW certification courses will be held with fewer participants to ensure social distancing
  • Signs will be posted detailing the maximum number of people allowed on the premises at one time
  • AWENS has hired cleaning service that deep cleans the entire office two days a week
  • All high-touch surfaces are disinfected at least twice a day and more frequently as needed. High touch surfaces include items such as door handles, counters, cabinet doors, light switches, touch screen surfaces, and keypads
