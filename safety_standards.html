AWENS Safety Standards

Updated: September 1st, 2020

Providing Security

In light of the Covid-19 pandemic AWENS has raised our(or their) standards even higher. We will continue to provide the best in adult education practices while staying committed to your health and safety.

Our Plan

  • Our class rooms have been modified to ensure social distancing at all times.
  • Each training room has a separate enter and exit door to ensure a safe entr y and exit from every room
  • Gloves, masks, and sanitizer will be provided to instructors sand employees as necessary
  • Instructors and AWENS Employees will sanitize their hands before entering the training facility
  • Training rooms will be sanitized before and after each class
  • Our classes and ISW certification courses will be held with fewer participants to ensure social distancing
  • Any provided course materials will be thoroughly sanitized before and after use
  • Our courses and certification classes will be altered to ensure the safety of our safe and customers
  • Signs will be posted detailing the maximum number of people allowed on the premises at one time
  • AWENS has hired cleaning ser vice that deep cleans the entire office two days a week
  • All high-touch surfaces are disinfected at least twice a day and more frequently as needed. High touch surfaces include items such as door handles, counters, cabinet doors, light switches, touch screen surfaces, and keypads
