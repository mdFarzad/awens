Corporate Education Solutions


Access Subject Matter Experts Through AWENS Instructors Pipeline

At AWENS, we offer the opportunity to connect workplace education instructors working in a wide range of industry sectors to organizations that need to build capacity. AWENS' RFP process is a no fee service to corporations looking to find the right educational fit.

Our Values

Accessible We make workplace education and training accessible by connecting organization to federal and provincial funding programs, and matching them with instructors who are subject matters experts within their field
Accountable We pride ourselves on our accountability and reporting. We stand behind our prequalified talent pool of educators and faciliators and reliable screening mechanism in place to ensure quality
Knowledgeable Our extensive membership of subject matter experts work across many industry sectors and provide a high level of adult education and training best practices
Flexible In addition to offering customized programs tailored to each organization's spcific needs, we provide a diverse group of qualified educators, and make our resources available through flexible class times
Relevant AWENS' members are active in their field of practice, bringing a practical current approach that is relevant to today's workplace
AWENS has created a network of industry partners that allows us to identify key trends in workplace education and training. Our team integrates those concepts into our platform, keeping our course materials current and our training competitive. AWENS is here to bring world class, customized education and training to the workplace.

AWENS provides organizations looking to submit requests for proposals (RFPs) for education and training the services of qualified adult educators.

Professional Training


At AWENS, we offer a wide range of education and training programs that benefit both individual employees and business owners. As a non-profit organization, we offer the RFP process at no cost to encourage education among Nova Scotians in the workplace.

Our Instructors


The ability to offer quality education and training is a key component of the support and ser vices AWENS provides. In order to reach thousands of workers across the province, AWENS works with over 80 instructors and training consultants. AWENS' certified instructors are screened through a qualification process to ensure they have industr y experience as well as adult education knowledge and skills to deliver programs. Our instructors have diverse backgrounds, with many having earned Masters in Adult Education, Masters in Business Administration, PhD or a technical certification.
