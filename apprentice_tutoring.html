Apprenticeship Tutoring


Small group and one-on-one tutoring for Red Seal Exam & Apprenticeships Blocks

AWENS offers small group and one-on-one apprenticeship tutoring. Our learning coaches develop specific learning plans for personal education needs. Whether trying to ace a Red Seal exam or Apprenticeship blocks, AWENS can help.

The Tutoring Process


  • Define Goals

    Step 1: Define Goals


    The student and learning coach identify the student’s educational needs and goals. In addition, establish time constraints and scheduling.

  • Path Ahead

    Step 2: The Path Ahead


    Based on the student's assessment, the instructor and student identify focus areas, plan a sequence of topics, and finalize a study program.

  • Carry out the Learning Program

    Step 3: Carry Out The Learning Program


    Student and coach implement the study program, working towards the student's educational goals.

  • Test and Assess

    Step 4: Test And Assess


    At the end of the study program the student and the coach will assess the readiness of the student to move forward and write the exam.
