Workplace Education Initiative

AWENS works to support Labour and Advanced Education’s Workplace Education Initiative (WEI). WEI works in partnership with business, industry, and labour to support the development of a skilled and adaptable workforce, improving workplace growth and essential skills and helping businesses manage workplace change. WEI is a nationally recognized program aimed at improving workplace essential skills and managing skills transitions. Many of AWENS members delivered WEI programs to small businesses through full ‘cluster programs’, which are hosted by business associations.

https://novascotia.ca/lae/workplace-education/

Labour and Advanced Education

AWENS and the Department of Labour and Advanced Education (LAE) have been strategic partners for over 20 years. AWENS instructors strongly support the objectives of the provincial government's Workplace Education Initiative program. Since 1999, AWENS has worked collaboratively with LAE to provide support services (administrative function) to AWENS member instructors and LAE clients.

https://novascotia.ca/lae/

ISW Network

AWENS in partnership with higher education institutes across Canada supports the work of a very successful professional development initiative for educators -- the Instructional Skills Workshop (ISW). Since its creation in Canada (1979), hundreds of educators and institutions have adopted ISW as an instructional standard.

https://www.iswnetwork.ca/
